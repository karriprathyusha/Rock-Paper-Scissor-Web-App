How to play

Start a match by clicking START. Then pick ROCK, PAPER, or SCISSOR. The computer will reveal its choice and the winner of that round earns one point.

Match format: First to 3 points wins the match.

After a decisive round (non-draw) the result will remain visible until you click CONTINUE. Draws automatically proceed to the next round.

Game Details
  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Match draw awards no points
